About Us

Welcome to our Welfare Organization

At our Welfare Organization, we are committed to fighting food waste and hunger by creating a seamless connection between surplus food providers and NGOs. Our innovative platform bridges the gap between restaurants, community halls, and volunteers, ensuring that excess food reaches those in need rather than ending up in landfills. By fostering collaboration and community engagement, we aim to create a sustainable system that benefits everyone involved.

Our mission is to reduce food waste, support local communities, and provide safe, nutritious meals to vulnerable populations. We believe in the power of technology and collective effort to drive meaningful change. Through our platform, we offer real-time notifications, geolocation services, and a community forum to share strategies on reducing food waste. Join us in our mission to make a tangible difference, one meal at a time. Together, we can build a more sustainable and compassionate world.
